Welcome to ESM

Environmental Science (M) Sdn. Bhd. (ESM) is a dynamic and innovative company providing environmental and occupational testing and consulting services to a diverse clientele that includes a large number of well known multi-nationals, local industries and public agencies. For more than two decade, we have provided substantial field and laboratory support to many important development projects such as Putrajaya, KL International Airport, power plants, steel mills, solid and hazardous waste disposal sites, paper mills, highways and all forms of land development.

Our reputation is built upon quality, value added and highly competent services that meet the environmental, occupational and safety objectives of our clients. We have highly experienced personnel in the fields of analytical and environmental chemistry, ecology, geology, environmental management sciences and engineering. Coupled with our full-service and ISO 17025 certified testing laboratory, our clients are assured of quality and competent services with full confidence and satisfaction.

Foreword Message

On behalf of the Board of Directors, it gives me a great pleasure to present the newly redesigned ESM website, a testament to our highly competent services.

Environmental Science (M) Sdn. Bhd. has been established for more than two decades. In line with the company’s tagline, “The solutions provider for a greener environment”, ESM aims to address environmental issues by providing high standards monitoring and consulting services in order to reverse some of the destructive forces and heal the environment.

Along those successful years, we have extended our services by opening four sisters companies known as E S Laboratories (M) Sdn. Bhd., ES Techventure Sdn. Bhd., EST Supplies Sdn. Bhd. as well as ES EcoSmart Sdn. Bhd. These companies cater a diverse range of industrial and commercial clients. Through our commitment to safety, health and environmental excellence, we are now a proud owner of a seven storey building recognized as “Menara ES”. This is in hand with our aspiration of becoming a one-stop centre in the environmental industry.

ES Group has been involved in various projects both locally and internationally. To date, we have provided a variety of field and laboratory support to many important development projects such as MRT (Klang Valley Mass Rapid Transit Project) SBK and SSP Lines, LRT 1, 2 and 3 (Light Rail Transit), Putrajaya (Malaysia’s governmental administrative capital), Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA), companies from the oil and gas industry, a variety of manufacturing industries (power plants, steel mills, paper mills, quarries), solid and hazardous waste disposal sites, building and highway construction companies, and all forms of land development firms.

With that being said, this website will be able to let you know more about our company, provide substantial information on our latest developments projects as well as in depth explanation on a range of services that we provide to you.

In short, thank you for your continuous support to our companies and we look forward to providing you with the best service in the future.

ESM Time Capsule
  • AUGUST
    1992
    Environmental Science (M) Sdn. Bhd. was founded by the late Mr. Soo Ah Kan, Tuan Haji Zulkifli Dato Haji Ahmad and Ms. Felicia Choo Boon Lian. They humbly started by occupying a single room office located at Medan Damansara and assisted by one admin staff.
    OCTOBER
    E S Laboratories (M) Sdn. Bhd. was established and it was spearheaded by Ms. Felicia Choo Boon Lian and assisted by 2 lab technicians.
  • AUGUST
    1993
    Moved to a 1 Floor office in Bukit Emas, Kelana Jaya and had 3 additional staff.
  • OCTOBER
    1995
    Moved to 1 ½ terrace factory in Jalan Tembaga, Sri Damansara.
  • DECEMBER
    1997
    Environmental Science (M) Sdn. Bhd. opened up their first branch in Penang.
  • NOVEMBER
    1998
    Bought a Semi-D factory at Jalan Tago, Sri Damansara and moved our operation to a new double storey building.
  • AUGUST
    2004
    Second branch office in Gebeng, Kuantan was established.
  • OCTOBER
    2007
    ES Techventure Sdn. Bhd. established its own laboratory in Gebeng, Kuantan.
  • JANUARY
    2014
    We expanded our core business and bought over a hardware company in Gebeng, Kuantan. Known as EST Supplies Sdn. Bhd., specialises in supplying hardware materials cater customize maintenance equipment for factories surrounding Gebeng Industrial area.
  • MARCH
    2015
    ES Eco Smart Sdn. Bhd. was registered. This company specialises in EIA and due diligence.
    DECEMBER
    Environmental Science (M) Sdn. Bhd. opened its third branch in Johor Bahru.
  • JUNE - CURRENT
